ABOUT MEMBERS FACTS
ABOUT
Rammstein is German metal band formed in Berlin in 1994. The band members have remained unchanged throughout their existence. Rammstein has achieved particular fame for their over-the-top stage show, making use of extensive pyrotechnics. Lindemann is even a licensed pyrotechnician and has suffered multiple burns on his ears, head and arms.
Richard Zven Kruspe
Born on the 24 June 1967. He is the bands guitarist and the frontman of the band Emigrate.
Till Lindemann
Born on the 4 January 1963 is a leadsinger, songwriter, musician, actor, poet and pyrotechnican. He is noted for his muscular stature, bass voice, and unique stage performances.
Christian Lorenz
Was born 16 November 1966. He is the bands Keyboard player. Lorenz commonly goes by the nicknames "Flake" In a 16 December 2000 interview, he stated that "Flake" is his proper name.
Christoph Schneider
Born 11 May 1966 is a German musician. He is the drummer of the band. the nickname "Doom" comes from the video game of the same name. Paul Landers suggested the name "Doom" because they liked the game.
Paul H.Landers
Was born 9 December 1964 and is a guitarist for the band Rammstein, and the punk rock band Feeling B.
Oliver Riedel
Was born on the 11 April 1971, he is a bassist in the band. Ridel is primarily a fingerstyle player, although he uses a pick for most songs when playing live.
FACTS
Albums
Rammstein has released 7 albums since 1995 to 2019, with the latest being called Untitled, whish was their first studio album in ten years, it is their first not to be produced by Jacob Hellner.
Tours
Rammstein has had 10 tours since 1994, and are touring for the 11th time all over Europe. The on ongoing tour is called “Europe Stadium Tour”.
Awards
Rammstein has been nominated and has won several awards in their long careers as a band. including a grammy award nomination for their hit song "Du hast".
